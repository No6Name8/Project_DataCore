SECTION 6: LIMITATIONS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  - Real Saudi SME transaction data unavailable publicly
  - UCI dataset represents UK wholesale retail (2009-2011)
  - Amount normalisation applied for cross-currency comparison;
    absolute amounts are scaled, behavioral patterns are not
  - Supermarket dataset limited to 1,000 transactions (Kaggle sample)
  - Car Dealer and General Retail CSVs are subsampled (10k and 50k rows);
    avg_daily_transactions corrected by 1/sampling_fraction to estimate
    true business density. Car Dealer CSV represents aggregated multi-dealer
    data, so velocity tag may not reflect a single SME dealership.
  - Three datasets (Car Dealer, Real Estate, General Retail) have date-only
    timestamps; temporal features (night_ratio, hour_entropy) set to neutral.
  - Validation conducted on datasets from different regions
    and time periods than target deployment context (Saudi Arabia 2025)
  - Car dealer dataset is synthetic despite realistic price ranges
