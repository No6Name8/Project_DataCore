# DataCore — iz Business

## Product Purpose
Real-time AI credit assessment platform for Saudi SMEs. Replaces static loan applications with live POS transaction data, energy logs, and behavioral AI scoring. Built for Alinma Bank's iz Business hackathon.

## Users
- **Loan officers** reviewing SME credit applications (primary)
- **SME owners** checking their own credit profile and limits
- **New entrepreneurs** exploring salary-backed seed loans (Incubator pipeline)

## Register
product

## Brand
- Deep navy blue #1B3A6B as the dominant surface color
- Gold #C9A84C as the sole accent — used for CTAs, highlights, data emphasis
- Dark background #0F1923 — the product is used in office environments, often on large monitors
- Tone: premium, trustworthy, precise. Never playful. Never startup-casual.
- Bilingual: English primary, Arabic (RTL) secondary — both must feel native

## Anti-references
- No neon fintech (Revolut neon green, crypto dashboard aesthetic)
- No light-mode banking apps (HSBC white/red, traditional bank UIs)
- No AI dashboard clichés (purple gradients, robot icons, "powered by AI" badges)
- No startup SaaS (Notion-style white, pastel accent colors)

## Strategic Principles
- Data must feel authoritative: numbers should be crisp, readable, never decorative
- Trustworthiness over delight: conservative animations, nothing flashy
- Every chart and metric must justify its presence — no vanity stats
- Saudi cultural context: Arabic RTL layouts must feel as native as English LTR
